Just testing About!

Professional (cv): I have a degree in Geography from the University of Granada. The last year of my career I was given an Erasmus scholarship to France, but I finally opted for a Seneca/SICUE scholarship in Salamanca, where I finished the degree. I have worked as an Expert in GIS since 2006 and since 2014, as an Expert in GIS and Remote Sensing. I came to Python by chance, as a tool to program loops in ArcGIS. With the passage of time and many courses and some books, Python has become my main working tool, replacing ArcGIS first and QGIS later. I know something about HTML, Javascript and CSS (and very little Java, which I also forget very quickly). I dream of learning R, but I still have so much to learn from Python that I can't find time for it. I would not say that I have a very high Python skills, but at least I can solve the daily problems (puzzles) that pose to me.
