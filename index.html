For a long time, I wanted to make a web page where I could carry out a kind of tracking of the python scripts that I do in my daily work at the GIS and Remote Sensing Laboratory of the Donana Biological Station.
There came a time when the amount of Python Notebooks exceeded my memory, which together with my chaotic and poorly organized character (also, i am not very good in self-marketing) makes me occasionally see myself repeating some script, that I know I have done before, but that I am not able to find. The idea of this website, therefore, is to make a "diary" in which I explain myself the scripts I am doing. If at the same time, someone can take profite of those scripts, welcome.

So, in just one line: This web is about Python applied to Remote Sensing and GIS

Finally, why in English if I am a Spanish native speaker who barely knows English? (did I say I am bad in self-marketing?) I don’t know why the hell I do that, but whenever I code I always write comments in English. So, somehow it's natural.
